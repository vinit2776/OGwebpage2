About OneGrid

Built by engineers
who got tired of
unread spreadsheets.

OneGrid started when our founders — ex-grid operators and energy analysts — noticed the same pattern at every industrial facility they visited: the data to save crores was already there. Nobody was reading it.

We built the intelligence layer that should have existed. Today we help C&I facility managers across Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Telangana, and Gujarat stop losing money on energy they've already bought.

OneGrid is also a licensed Independent Power Producer. When the platform identifies that your facility would benefit from captive solar, we can provide it — directly, without a third party.
